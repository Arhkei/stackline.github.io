Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Oasis Labs (“Company,” “we,” or “us”) processes personal data in connection with our application (the “App”) on the Clover Point of Sale system (“Clover POS”).

## 1. Information We Collect
Oasis Labs is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from Merchants or their Customers.

Transaction & Payment Data: We do not see or store credit card numbers, transaction amounts, or payment details.

Customer & Merchant Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or employee information.

Automated Collection: Our App does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or background analytics to identify users.

## 2. How We Use Information
Because we do not collect personal data, we do not use, sell, or rent any information to third parties. Any processing that occurs happens locally on the Clover device to provide the App's functionality and is not transmitted to Oasis Labs.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not share personal data because we do not possess it. We may only disclose information if required by a legal obligation or a lawful request from government authorities, though we would have no user data to provide in such a scenario.

## 4. Your Rights and Choices
Data Subject Rights: To the extent that applicable laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) provide individuals with rights to review or delete their personal information, such requests should be directed to the Merchant (the data controller) or Clover. Since Oasis Labs does not store any personal data, we cannot provide, move, or delete data we do not have.

California Privacy Rights: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. Oasis Labs has not sold or disclosed any consumer personal information in the preceding 12 months, as no such data is collected.

## 5. Data Retention
We do not maintain databases of merchant or customer information; therefore, we have no data retention period for personal information.

## 6. Updates to This Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of updates by changing the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
